An italian language center to learn culture and language in a fun, safe and stimulating environment

Spazio Italiano is an Italian language center for children and their families located in San Francisco ( North Beach). We offer full Italian immersion programs for kids 9 months to 16 years of age as well as parent participation in an intimate, well designed space.

An authentic learning experience with Native Italian Instructors Founded in 2004

Since 2004 our focus at Spazio Italiano has been to provide a consistent space where children and their families can learn the Italian language and connect with the traditions and cultural heritage of Italy.
